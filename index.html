About Me

Hi, I’m Muhammad Danial Javed, a passionate Mechanical Engineer with a strong interest in renewable energy, robotics, and advanced manufacturing systems. I graduated from the University of Engineering and Technology (UET) Lahore, where I built a solid foundation in mechanical design, FEM, and energy systems, alongside practical experience in cutting-edge technologies like CAD, CAE, and data-driven optimization.


Currently, I’m working at Pak-Arab Refinery (PARCO), where I’ve honed my expertise in mechanical systems, project management, and operational efficiency. My projects range from optimizing large-scale industrial processes to designing innovative solutions like a 3D-printed prosthetic hand and a solar-powered seawater desalination plant.


Beyond engineering, I’m passionate about leveraging technology to solve real-world problems, especially in renewable energy and sustainability. My work is driven by a desire to address critical challenges such as clean energy access and the water crisis in regions like Thar, Pakistan. As I move forward in my career, I aim to contribute to the global transition towards sustainable energy systems, with a focus on innovative design and efficiency.
